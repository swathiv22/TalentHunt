Frequently Asked Questions

Talent Hunt is an online platform that connects talented individuals with agencies seeking new talent. We act as an intermediary, facilitating the process of discovery, communication, and collaboration between talent and agencies.

Talents create profiles showcasing their skills and portfolios, while agencies search for and contact talents for potential opportunities. Our platform ensures a smooth and secure interaction, providing tools for messaging, contracts, and payments.

Click on the "Sign Up" button on our homepage and fill the required details.

Ensure your profile is complete and up-to-date. Upload high-quality portfolio items, write a compelling bio, and actively check and respond to messages from agencies.

Payments are processed through our secure payment system. Once a contract is completed, the agreed-upon amount will be transferred to your account. Details on payment methods and schedules can be found in our Payment Policy.

Please report any inappropriate behavior or suspected scams to our support team immediately. We take such reports seriously and will investigate and take appropriate action.
